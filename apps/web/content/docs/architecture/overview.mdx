## Two layers

- **OS surfaces** — `os-desktop`, `os-cli`, `os-flow`, `os-triggers`. User-facing.
- **OS services** — `os-security`, `os-marketplace`, `os-mcp-bridge`, `os-cloud-sync`. Behind the surfaces.

Both depend on `@agentskit/os-core`. Nothing in the OS layer talks to AgentsKit
internals directly except through `@agentskit/os-runtime-agentskit`.

  traces/                    # span exports
