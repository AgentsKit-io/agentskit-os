## Process

RFCs are discussed in PRs against `docs/rfc/`. Anyone can open one. Acceptance
requires sign-off from at least one maintainer plus a successful test plan
documented inside the RFC.

See [`docs/RFC-PROCESS.md`](https://github.com/AgentsKit-io/agentskit-os/blob/main/docs/RFC-PROCESS.md).
